SINGAPORE</td></tr></table><div class="prov1"><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov1Hdr" id="pr9-"><span class="noBold"></span><span class="">Definitions of this Part</span></td></tr></table><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov1Txt"><strong>9.</strong>&#xA0;&#xA0;In this Part, unless the context otherwise requires&#160;&#8212;<table width="100%"><tr><td class="def"  style="font-size:13pt">“data in transit” means personal data transferred through Singapore in the course of onward transportation to a country or territory outside Singapore, without the personal data being accessed or used by, or disclosed to, any organisation (other than the transferring organisation or an employee of the transferring organisation acting in the course of the employee’s employment with the transferring organisation) while the personal data is in Singapore, except for the purpose of such transportation;</td></tr></table><table width="100%"><tr><td class="def"  style="font-size:13pt">“individual’s personal data” means personal data about an individual;</td></tr></table><table width="100%"><tr><td class="def"  style="font-size:13pt">“recipient”, in relation to personal data transferred from Singapore to a country or territory outside Singapore, means any organisation that receives in a country or territory outside Singapore the personal data transferred to it by or on behalf of the transferring organisation, but does not include&#160;&#8212;<table class="p1_1"  style="font-size:13pt" width="100%"><tr><td class="p1DefNo">(<em>a</em>)</td><td class="pDefTxt">the transferring organisation;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1DefNo">(<em>b</em>)</td><td class="pDefTxt">any employee of the transferring organisation acting in the course of the employee’s employment with that organisation;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1DefNo">(<em>c</em>)</td><td class="pDefTxt">any organisation that receives the personal data solely as a network service provider or carrier; or</td></tr><tr><td class="p1DefNo">(<em>d</em>)</td><td class="pDefTxt">any organisation that receives the personal data from a recipient of that personal data;</td></tr></table></td></tr></table><table width="100%"><tr><td class="def"  style="font-size:13pt">“transferring organisation”&#160;&#8212;<table class="p1_1"  style="font-size:13pt" width="100%"><tr><td class="p1DefNo">(<em>a</em>)</td><td class="pDefTxt">in relation to any personal data transferred from Singapore to a country or territory outside Singapore, means the organisation that transfers the personal data from Singapore to the country or territory outside Singapore; or</td></tr><tr><td class="p1DefNo">(<em>b</em>)</td><td class="pDefTxt">in relation to data in transit, means the organisation that transfers the personal data through Singapore to the country or territory outside Singapore;</td></tr></table></td></tr></table><table width="100%"><tr><td class="def"  style="font-size:13pt">“transportation” includes transmission in electronic form.</td></tr></table></td></tr></table></div><div class="prov1"><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov1Hdr" id="pr10-"><span class="noBold"></span><span class="">Requirements for transfer</span></td></tr></table><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov1Txt"><strong>10.</strong><a name="pr10-ps1-"></a><span class="prov2TxtIL">&#8212;(1)&#xA0;&#xA0;For the purposes of section&#160;26 of the Act, a transferring organisation must, before transferring an individual’s personal data to a country or territory outside Singapore on or after 1 February 2021, take appropriate steps to ascertain whether, and to ensure that, the recipient of the personal data is bound by legally enforceable obligations (in accordance with regulation&#160;11) to provide to the transferred personal data a standard of protection that is at least comparable to the protection under the Act.</span><a name="pr10-ps2-"></a><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov2Txt">(2)&#xA0;&#xA0;A transferring organisation is taken to have satisfied the requirements of paragraph&#160;(1) in respect of an individual’s personal data which it transfers to a recipient in a country or territory outside Singapore if&#160;&#8212;<table class="p1_1"  style="font-size:13pt" width="100%"><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>a</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">subject to paragraph&#160;(3), the individual consents to the transfer of the individual’s personal data to that recipient in that country or territory;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>b</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">the individual is deemed to have consented to the disclosure by the transferring organisation of the individual’s personal data to that recipient under section&#160;15(3), (4), (5), (6), (7) or (8) of the Act;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>c</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">the transfer of the personal data to the recipient is necessary for the personal data to be used or disclosed under Part&#160;1 or paragraph&#160;2 of Part&#160;2 of the First&#160;Schedule to the Act, and the transferring organisation has taken reasonable steps to ensure that the personal data so transferred will not be used or disclosed by the recipient for any other purpose;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>d</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">the personal data is data in transit; or</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>e</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">the personal data is publicly available in Singapore.</td></tr></table></td></tr></table><a name="pr10-ps3-"></a><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov2Txt">(3)&#xA0;&#xA0;For the purposes of paragraph&#160;(2)(<em>a</em>), an individual is not taken to have consented to the transfer of the individual’s personal data to a country or territory outside Singapore if&#160;&#8212;<table class="p1_1"  style="font-size:13pt" width="100%"><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>a</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">the individual was not, before giving his or her consent, given a reasonable summary in writing of the extent to which the personal data to be transferred to that country or territory will be protected to a standard comparable to the protection under the Act;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>b</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">the binding instrument.</td></tr></table></span><a name="pr11-ps2-"></a><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov2Txt">(2)&#xA0;&#xA0;A contract mentioned in paragraph&#160;(1)(<em>b</em>) must&#160;&#8212;<table class="p1_1"  style="font-size:13pt" width="100%"><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>a</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">require the recipient to provide a standard of protection for the personal data transferred to the recipient that is at least comparable to the protection under the Act; and</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>b</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">specify the countries and territories to which the personal data may be transferred under the contract.</td></tr></table></td></tr></table><a name="pr11-ps3-"></a><table width="100%"><tr><td class="prov2Txt">(3)&#xA0;&#xA0;The provide a standard of protection for the personal data transferred to the recipient that is at least comparable to the protection under the Act;</td></tr><tr><td class="p1No">(<em>b</em>)</td><td class="pTxt">must specify&#160;&#8212;<table class="p2_1"  style="font-size:13pt" width="100%"><tr id="pr11-ps3-p1b-p2i-"><td class="p2No">(i)</td><td class="p2Txt">the recipients of the transferred
